Fundamentals of well testing

Why Do We Test Wells? The main reason for testing an exploration well is to take a fluid sample. Further reasons are to measure the initial pressure, estimate a minimum reservoir volume, evaluate the well permeability and skin effect, and identify heterogeneities and boundaries.

Drilling engineering is a subset of petroleum engineering. Drilling engineers design and implement procedures to drill wells as safely and economically as possible.
